# Personal Note

```
Mon cher Édouard,

Aujourd'hui, en rangeant le grenier chez mes grands-parents, je suis tombée sur cette vieille coupure de journal. Ton arrière-grand-père n'avait même pas l'âge de passer le permis quand il s'est distingué ce jour-là. Le benjamin de l'équipe, et certainement pas le moins courageux.

Il serait si fier de te voir sur l'eau à ton tour.

Avec toute mon affection,
Audette
```

Sure its English translation is 

```
My dear Édouard,

Today, while tidying up the attic at my grandparents’ house, I came across this old newspaper clipping. Your great-grandfather wasn’t even old enough to get a driver’s license when he distinguished himself that day. The youngest member of the team, and certainly not the least courageous.

He would be so proud to see you out on the water in your turn.

With all my affection,
Audette
```

Audette(a female name) is writting to Édouard about a newspaper clipping she found into her grandparents home. and that was definetly about the grand-grandfater of Édouard.
